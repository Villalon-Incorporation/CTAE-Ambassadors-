Your guide to the GCA PBIS Store via Clever.

How to access the PBIS Store via Clever

Follow these simple steps to get to the PBIS Store and spend your well-earned points!

Step 1: Log in to GCA Clever

Log in to your GCA Clever account using your student credentials.

Step 2: Find and Click the PBIS Store App

Once logged in, scroll through your applications and click on the PBIS Store icon.

Step 3: Browse and Shop!

You're in! Use your points to redeem awesome rewards.

PBIS Tips and What they can be used for

Tips for earning PBIS points

Stay on task in class, participate in discussions, and always show a positive attitude! Your teachers love to see you engaged.

What are my PBIS points used for?

Your PBIS points can be redeemed for a variety of rewards, including virtual prizes, gift cards, and exclusive GCA merchandise. Check the store for all the possibilities!
